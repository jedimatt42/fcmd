# Links to the TI world

means have a look at [Rich Polivka's site](http://www.99er.net). It is a
gold mine of information, most of which is easier to understand than the
"geek-chow" you'll find here.

In addition, Rich has a large link-page, with links to almost every
website dedicated to the TI-99 (4 and 4A). It would be pointless for me
to reproduce (part of) it in this page, so I guess I'll just refer you
to it. Just follow [this link](http://www.99er.net/links/).
